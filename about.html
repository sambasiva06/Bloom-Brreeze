About Bloom & Brreeze

More than a café — a space built for comfort, creativity, and connection.

Since 2026

A Vision of Pure Connection

“Bloom & Breeze began with a simple idea — to create a space where people can slow down and feel at ease.”

Founded by six passionate women, our café is built on warmth, connection, and the joy of sharing moments over coffee.

What started as a dream on February 14, 2026, is now a place where every visit feels personal and every cup is made with care.
