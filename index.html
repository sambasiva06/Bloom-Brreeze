Our Story

Bloom & Breeze began with a simple idea — to create a space where people can slow down and feel at ease. Our community often calls us Bloom Breeze or Bloom & Brreeze, and we love every version of it.

Founded by six passionate women, our café is built on warmth, connection, and the joy of sharing moments over coffee.
